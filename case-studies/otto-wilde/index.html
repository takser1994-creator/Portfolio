About Project

Otto Wilde is a premium smart grilling platform that empowers outdoor cooking enthusiasts to take their grilling experience to the next level. This project focused on designing a seamless, intuitive mobile app that connects users to their Otto grills, offering full control, recipe guidance, and smart cooking features— all in one place.

Core Features

The Otto Wilde Grill app brings precision and control to your outdoor cooking.

500+ grill-optimized recipes

Session analytics and gas usage tracking

Built-in shopping list feature

Direct/indirect heat zone control

User Interview Insights

User interviews revealed key grilling challenges, habits and needs that shaped our design decisions.

Pro users

Based on user interviews, grilling isn’t just about cooking — it’s about learning.
Users want to track temperature, compare sessions, and review fuel usage to understand what worked and how to repeat it.
Meat
Fish
Vegetables

Recipe customisation

During interviews, users emphasized the importance of personalization. They want to save their own recipes and adjust steps to match their cooking style, building a library that reflects how they like to grill.

Gas level monitor

Users want a gas level monitor to avoid unexpected interruptions while cooking. Many have experienced running out of gas mid-session, which ruins the flow and timing of a meal—especially when hosting guests.

Problem and Solution

We uncovered key problems users face while grilling and designed focused solutions to solve them through better guidance and clarity.

Solution

Easy Setup & Smart Connection

The app walks users through a quick setup and connects smoothly to the grill via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth.

Live Temperature Monitoring

Users can view real-time grill and food temperatures with clear visuals and instant alerts.

Smart Cooking Support

Step-by-step guidance tailored to your grill, helping you cook with confidence from prep to plate.

Problem

Complex Setup & Connectivity

Connecting the grill to the app is confusing, with little guidance — especially challenging for first-time users.

Unclear Temperature Tracking

Users can’t easily track grill or food temperature in real time, leading to guesswork and poor results.

No Interactive Cooking Support

The app offers no built-in recipes, leaving users without guidance tailored to their grill setup.

Impact & Results

Post-launch data showed clear improvements in user confidence, cooking success, and engagement

+35% User Engagement

After launch, users spent significantly more time in the app, especially during active grilling sessions.

+30% in Setup Stability

Improvements to onboarding and connection flows led to a more reliable setup process, reflected in a 30% boost to the stability index.

Fewer Support Requests

Fewer users reached out to support for setup or connection issues, thanks to a smoother and more intuitive experience.

+24% Recipe Feature Usage

The new guided cooking feature became one of the most-used parts of the app, showing high user interest in integrated recipes.

+25% Higher User Satisfaction

Support inquiries related to setup and connection issues decreased significantly, driven by a more intuitive onboarding experience and improved in-app guidance.
